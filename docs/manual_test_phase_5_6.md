# Manual Test Checklist - Phase 5 + Phase 6

Use a fresh or disposable save if possible.

## Phase 5

- Town Construction: start a level 1 building, confirm the timer appears, accelerate with Focus, cancel and verify an 80% material refund with no Focus refund.
- Construction Claim: let/skip a construction to ready, claim it, and confirm building level and daily building progress update.
- Caravan: start a regional Caravan, confirm expeditions are blocked while it is active, then claim it for Gold, regional material, Account XP, and focus-specific reward.
- Outpost: defeat a region boss, open Region Completion, assign one Outpost bonus, then switch it to another bonus.
- Class Change: after some progress but before Rebirth, use the early free respec; later verify class change requires/uses Rebirth rules.

## Phase 6

- Traits: find or craft Rare+ items and confirm at most one visible trait pill appears on an item.
- Context Gear: use Equip Best on Bramblecrown and confirm Piercing/Guarded-style items are preferred when relevant, even if raw Item Power is lower.
- Families: equip 2-3 Sunlit Charter or Emberbound Kit items and confirm resonance appears in Inventory Build Presets.
- Presets: save Preset 1 and Preset 2, change gear, then equip the saved preset back.
- Locks: lock an inventory/equipped item and confirm Sell/Salvage are blocked until it is unlocked.
