Task candidates:

- Complete 3 expeditions.
- Win 2 expeditions.
- Defeat 1 boss.
- Salvage 3 items.
- Sell 3 items.
- Craft 1 item.
- Upgrade 1 building.
- Spend 20 Vigor.

- Pick exactly 3 unique tasks/day.
