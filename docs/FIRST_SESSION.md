# Relic Forge Idle - First Session Design (0 to 15 Minutes)

## Goal

Make the first 15 minutes feel rewarding and understandable with minimal friction.

## Target Timeline

### Minute 0 to 2

- Character creation (name + class).
- Start the first 12s expedition.
- First completion gives a guaranteed weapon drop.
- Result card shows the reward line, whether the item is better, and an equip action.

### Minute 2 to 5

- Run 25s and 45s expeditions.
- Reach the first level-up and unlock the first boss gate.
- Earn enough gold and ore to upgrade the first equipped item.
- Earn enough gold and ore to buy the first Forge building upgrade.
- Header goal points to the next best action instead of only the next dungeon lock.

### Minute 5 to 10

- Attempt the Copper Crown Champion boss milestone.
- Boss rewards fund the first craft even after one item upgrade and one town upgrade.
- Introduce Vigor boost usage.

### Minute 10 to 15

- Reach first boss clear or be one step away with a clear objective.
- Complete at least one daily task.
- Use the Forge once through either item upgrade or craft.

## UX Messaging Requirements

- Always show:
  - current expedition status
  - time remaining
  - success chance
  - next recommended target
- Reward panel must clearly show what changed.
- Equip, item upgrade, and town upgrade messages must show Power delta when Power changes.
- Boss milestone should be visible before the player can start it.

## Acceptance Criteria

- New player can start first expedition within 20 seconds of app load.
- New player can complete at least 3 expeditions in first 5 minutes.
- New player sees first meaningful upgrade decision (equip, forge, or building) by minute 5.
- New player can afford one equipped item upgrade and one Forge building upgrade by minute 5 if the first three expeditions succeed.
- New player can afford one craft by minute 10 to 15 after the first boss reward, even after the first item and town upgrades.
- New player sees clear reincarnation progress direction before minute 15.

## Done When

- First-session flow can be played without confusion.
- At least one short-term and one medium-term goal are visible by minute 15.
